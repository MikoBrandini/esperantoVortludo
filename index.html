Unique Consonants
Letter IPA Symbol Like in...
Cc [ts] cats, pizza
Ĉĉ [t͡ʃ] cheerios, ketchup
Gg [ɡ] good, goats
Ĝĝ [d͡ʒ] gem, jump
Ĥĥ [x] loch, yech(disgust!)
Jj [j] yes, young
Ĵĵ [ʒ] pleasure, vision
Rr [r] r (trilled, but the American r ([ɹ]) is understood)
Ŝŝ [ʃ] shampoo, polish
Ŭŭ [ʃ] weather, mostly used in vowel combinations(aŭ, eŭ)

Vowels
Letter IPA Symbol Like in...
Aa [a] cot, spa
Ee [e] bet, sweat
Ii [i] machine, free
Oo [o] fork, pork
These are actually: [ɔ]. American English doesn't have [o]
Uu [u] boot, compute
Same pronounciation as in English: Bb, Dd, Ff, Hh, Kk, Ll, Mm, Nn, Pp, Ss, Tt, Vv, Zz
